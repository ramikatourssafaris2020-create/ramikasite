Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?
Use the booking form on our homepage or send us a WhatsApp/call. No sign-in required — just fill in your details and we'll confirm within 24 hours.
Are prices per person?
Yes, all listed prices are per person. Group discounts are available — contact us directly to discuss your group size and we'll tailor a package.
Do you offer custom itineraries?
Absolutely. We specialize in tailor-made safaris. Tell us your interests, budget, and available dates and we'll build a custom itinerary just for you.
What's included in the tour price?
Prices typically include a local guide, park entry fees, and transport within the tour area. Accommodation and meals are specified per-tour. Contact us for a full breakdown.
What's the best time to visit?
For wildflower season at Kitulo, November through April is peak. For forest safaris, June through October offers excellent conditions. We operate year-round.
How do I pay for my tour?
After confirming your booking we'll send payment instructions. We accept mobile money (M-Pesa / Airtel), bank transfer, and cash at our Njombe office.
